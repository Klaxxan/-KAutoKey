About KAutoKey

KAutoKey is a lightweight Windows application designed to automate key presses on your keyboard. Perfect for gaming, testing, or repetitive tasks, it lets you configure custom key combinations and intervals to boost productivity or gameplay efficiency.

Features

  • Custom key combo support with Ctrl, Alt, Shift modifiers
  • Adjustable press intervals, including custom timing
  • Start/stop toggle key for quick control
  • Easy-to-use GUI with dropdowns and checkboxes
  • Lightweight and runs silently in the background

How to Use

  1. Select the key(s) you want to auto-press.
  2. Choose your preferred press interval or enter a custom value.
  3. Set a toggle keybind to start and stop the auto key presser easily.
  4. Press "Start" to begin auto pressing or use the toggle key during runtime.
  5. Press "Stop" or use the toggle key again to halt the automation.

Feel free to experiment with settings to fit your needs!
